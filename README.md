<h2 align="left">About me</h2>

###

<p align="left">
 Hi! I'm María, based in Madrid, and I studied Web Application Development (DAW). Over time, I discovered that my true passion lies in <strong>frontend development</strong>, where I can combine creativity and technology to bring ideas to life on the web.
</p>

<p align="left">
 I enjoy constantly learning, exploring new tools, and experimenting with different approaches to create attractive, accessible, and functional interfaces.
</p>

<p align="left">
 I’m currently working on personal projects and practicing with my favorite stack: <strong>Vue 3, Nuxt.js, TypeScript</strong>, as well as other technologies that help me grow as a developer.
</p>

<p align="left">
 My goal is to keep improving every day, provide creative solutions, and, in the near future, join a team where I can continue learning and adding value.
</p>
